Privacy Policy

DigiPass values your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard the data you provide while using our portal.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Personal details (Name, Age, Contact Number, Email ID)
  • Academic details (College ID, Roll Number, Admission Receipt, Course & Year)
  • Uploaded documents (College ID proof, Bonafide Certificate, Address proof)
  • Login credentials (username, password)
  • Application history (submitted concession forms, status updates)

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used only for:

  • Processing and verifying railway concession applications
  • Generating approved concession forms with digital signatures
  • Communicating updates about your application status
  • Maintaining secure student and admin accounts
  • Generating reports and analytics (admin use only)

3. Data Sharing & Security

  • DigiPass does not share your personal information with third parties.
  • Data is used only by authorized administrators for verification and approval.
  • Strong security measures (authentication, encrypted storage) protect student records.

4. User Rights

As a user, you have the right to:

  • Access and update your profile information
  • Upload or re-upload required documents
  • Reset your password through the "Forgot Password" feature
  • Request corrections if your data is incorrect

5. Cookies & Tracking

DigiPass does not use cookies or third-party tracking tools.

6. Changes to Privacy Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. Users will be notified on the website in case of major updates.
