Help & FAQ

Q1. What is DigiPass?

DigiPass is a digital portal where students can apply online for railway concession passes, track their application status, and download verified concession forms.

Q2. Who can apply for a railway concession?

Only registered students of D.G. Ruparel College (or affiliated institutions) who meet the eligibility criteria set by the railway authority.

Q3. What documents are required for applying?

You need a valid college ID card, admission receipt/bonafide certificate, and proof of residence.

Q4. How do I apply for a concession pass?

After logging in, go to the Apply for Concession section, fill in the required details, and upload the necessary documents.

Q5. Can I apply for multiple concessions in the same month?

No, duplicate applications for the same month are not allowed.

Q6. How can I check the status of my application?

Use the Track Status section in your dashboard to see whether your application is Pending, Approved, or Rejected.

Q7. What happens after my application is approved?

Once approved by the admin, you can download the digitally signed concession form and use it to buy your railway pass.

Q8. What if my application is rejected?

The rejection reason will be displayed in your dashboard. You can reapply after correcting the issues.

Q9. What is the role of the Admin?

The Admin verifies student applications, approves/rejects them, and issues digitally signed concession forms.

Q10. Can Admins generate reports?

Yes, admins can use the Reports & Analytics section to monitor student concession usage.

Q11. What should I do if I forget my password?

Use the Forgot Password option on the login page to reset your password via your registered email.

Q12. Is my personal data safe on DigiPass?

Yes, DigiPass follows strict data privacy policies. Your data is used only for concession processing and is not shared with third parties.
